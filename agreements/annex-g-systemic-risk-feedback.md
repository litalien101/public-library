# ANNEX G  
**Systemic Risk & Feedback Protocols**  
Guardian Override — Version 1.0  

---

## G.0 Purpose & Scope
- **G.0.1** Establishes standardized processes for identifying, reporting, and resolving systemic risks within Guardian Override.  
- **G.0.2** Defines structured feedback loops to ensure continuous improvement of moderation, enforcement, and contributor engagement.  
- **G.0.3** Designed to be audit‑ready, transparent, and globally enforceable.  

---

## G.1 Definitions
- **G.1.1 Systemic Risk** — Any recurring or structural issue that threatens platform integrity, contributor dignity, or community trust.  
- **G.1.2 Feedback Loop** — A structured mechanism for collecting, analyzing, and implementing stakeholder input.  
- **G.1.3 Escalation Path** — The defined route for raising risks from operational teams to governance review.  
- **G.1.4 Critical Threshold** — A risk classification requiring immediate governance intervention.  

---

## G.2 Risk Identification
- **G.2.1 Sources** — Moderator reports, contributor appeals, automated flagging, external audits.  
- **G.2.2 Thresholds** — Risks classified as Low, Moderate, or Critical based on recurrence and impact.  
- **G.2.3 Documentation** — All risks logged in Annex G dashboard template (see Appendix G.1).  
- **G.2.4 Verification** — Governance team validates risk classification within 72 hours of submission.

## G.3 Reporting Protocols
- **G.3.1 Moderators** — Submit risk reports within 24 hours of identification.  
- **G.3.2 Contributors** — May submit risk concerns via onboarding portal or appeal process.  
- **G.3.3 Governance Team** — Reviews aggregated reports weekly; escalates critical risks immediately.  
- **G.3.4 Publication** — Risk summaries published quarterly in contributor portal for transparency.

## G.4 Feedback Loops
- **G.4.1 Contributor Feedback** — Quarterly surveys benchmarked against Annex F protocols.  
- **G.4.2 Moderator Feedback** — Monthly debrief sessions with anonymized case studies.  
- **G.4.3 Governance Feedback** — Annual audit integrating Annex E enforcement outcomes.  
- **G.4.4 Continuous Learning** — Feedback incorporated into updated playbooks and onboarding flows.  

*(Placeholder: Circular diagram showing continuous feedback loop: Contributor → Moderator → Governance → System Update → Contributor)*

## G.5 Escalation & Resolution
- **G.5.1 Critical Risks** — Immediate governance review; provisional measures enacted within 48 hours.  
- **G.5.2 Moderate Risks** — Addressed within 30 days; resolution published in versioning log.  
- **G.5.3 Low Risks** — Incorporated into quarterly updates.  
- **G.5.4 Appeals** — Contributors may appeal risk classifications via Annex E appeal process.  

*(Placeholder: Decision tree diagram showing escalation paths by risk level)*

## G.6 Publication & Transparency
- **G.6.1 Versioning** — All updates logged in Annex G publication register.  
- **G.6.2 Accessibility** — Risk outcomes and feedback summaries published to contributor portal.  
- **G.6.3 Audit‑Readiness** — Annex G maintained in counsel‑ready format for VC/M&A review.  
- **G.6.4 Contributor Recognition** — Feedback incorporated into recognition protocols (see Annex H).
