# SOP-AI-2025: Handling AI Misuse

**Version:** v1.0  
**Effective Date:** 2025-11-30  
**Audience:** Contributors, Moderators, Governance Teams  
**Linked Annex:** Annex H — Conduct & Misuse Protocols  
**Status:** Binding  

---

## 1. Purpose
Define protocols for detecting, responding to, and preventing harmful AI-generated content.

## 2. Scope
Applies to contributors, moderators, and governance teams across all Guardian Override platforms.

---

## 3. Workflow

### 3.1 Identification
- Flag AI-generated outputs that are harmful (misinformation, harassment, impersonation, manipulative content).  
- Contributors must disclose AI tool and version used.

### 3.2 Classification
- **Minor:** Harmless but undisclosed AI use → advisory notice.  
- **Moderate:** Misleading or manipulative AI content → restriction.  
- **Critical:** Harmful, harassing, or impersonation content → suspension/ban.

### 3.3 Response
- Immediate suspension for Critical cases.  
- Escalation to Conduct Officers within 24h.  
- Documentation in MEI case log.

### 3.4 Restorative Pathways
- First-time offenders may undergo training on AI disclosure and ethics.

### 3.5 Audit
- Quarterly review of flagged AI cases.  
- Transparency report includes AI misuse statistics.

---

## 4. Compliance & Enforcement
Non-compliance results in enforcement actions per Annex H and MEI v1.0.  
All incidents must be logged and reviewed during quarterly audits.

---

## 5. Versioning & Updates
- **Change Log:** Record updates with date and rationale.  
- **Approval:** Governance Team approves updates.  
- **Notification:** Contributors notified via governance portal within 7 days.
