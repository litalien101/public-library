# SOP-Impersonation-Identity-Fraud

**Version:** v1.0  
**Effective Date:** 2025-11-30  
**Audience:** Moderators, Conduct Officers, Governance Teams  
**Linked Annex:** Annex E.2 — Identity Protocols  
**Status:** Binding  

---

## Purpose
Prevent impersonation of Guardian Override staff, contributors, or governance members.

## Scope
Applies across all platforms and partner ecosystems.

---

## Workflow

### Detection
- [ ] Reports of impersonation (fake staff accounts, fraudulent claims of authority).

### Immediate Action
- [ ] Suspend suspected account within 24h.  
- [ ] Escalate to Conduct Officers.

### Classification
- [ ] Minor: parody account clearly labeled → advisory.  
- [ ] Moderate: misleading identity → restriction.  
- [ ] Critical: fraudulent staff impersonation → suspension + law enforcement referral.

### Documentation
- [ ] Case logged in MEI Appendix E.2.  
- [ ] Transparency report includes impersonation cases.

### Audit
- [ ] Quarterly identity verification checks.
